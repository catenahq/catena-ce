# Catena Community License (fair-code)

Catena Community is **fair-code**: the source is published and free to
use, with one boundary -- you may not offer Catena to third parties as a
hosted or managed service.

Pending finalization with counsel, the intended terms follow the n8n
**Sustainable Use License** model:

- **Free** to run for your own internal business purposes and for
  non-commercial use, on infrastructure you control.
- **Source-available**: you may read, modify, and self-host the code.
- **Not** permitted: offering Catena (modified or not) to third parties
  as a hosted or managed service. That managed offering is Catena
  Business, the commercial edition.
- No time-based conversion to an OSI-approved open-source license (unlike
  BSL); this may be revisited.

Enterprise (Business) modules are **not** covered by this license. They
are distributed separately as compiled, license-gated binaries from
catenahq/catena-enterprise and are not published as source.

> with a Quebec software-licensing specialist before public release.
> Until then, treat this repository as source-available under the terms
> above.

Upstream applications deployed by Catena each carry their own license
(Nextcloud GPL, Rocket.Chat MIT, OnlyOffice AGPL, and so on); those are
unaffected by this file.
